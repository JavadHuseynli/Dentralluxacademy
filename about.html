The First Private Dentistry Training Center in Azerbaijan

Founded in 2021, Dentalux Academy in Baku has quickly become a leading institution for dental continuing education in Azerbaijan. Our mission is to provide dental professionals with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills through hands-on training programs.

Courses

We have conducted over 20 specialized courses, including implant-supported prosthetics, endodontics, aesthetic restoration, tooth design, and other dental specialties. Our courses combine theoretical knowledge with practical experience, allowing participants to work on models and real patients.

Masterclasses & Seminars

We collaborate with local and international experts to offer masterclasses and seminars, enhancing participants' practical skills and knowledge.

  • State-of-the-art dental equipment
  • Small classes for personalized learning
  • Hands-on training on models and real patients
  • Internationally recognized certifications
  • Workshops with expert dentists
