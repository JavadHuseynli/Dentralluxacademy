Advanced Implant Surgery Techniques
  • 23-24 May, 2025
  • 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • BAKU,Azerbaijan
  • 20 Seats
  • English

Course Description

This comprehensive two-day course is designed for dentists who want to enhance their implant surgery skills. Participants will learn advanced techniques for challenging cases, including immediate implant placement, bone grafting procedures, and management of complications.

The course includes both theoretical lectures and hands-on practical sessions with models, providing a complete learning experience.

Dr. Michael Brown

Implant Specialist with over 15 years of experience

Dr. Brown is a renowned implantologist who has performed over 5,000 implant surgeries. He is the author of numerous scientific articles and regularly lectures at international dental conferences.

Advanced Laminate Veneers
  • 28-29 May, 2025
  • 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • BAKU,Azerbaijan
  • 15 Seats
  • English

Course Description

This intensive two-day workshop focuses on the art and science of porcelain laminate veneers. Participants will learn contemporary preparation techniques, impression methods, and cementation protocols to achieve predictable and aesthetic results.

The course includes live demonstrations and hands-on practice sessions with models to ensure participants gain practical skills they can immediately apply in their practice.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Aesthetic Dentist specializing in ceramic restorations

Dr. Johnson has dedicated her career to aesthetic dentistry and is known for her meticulous approach to veneer preparation and placement. She lectures internationally and runs a successful aesthetic dental practice focusing on minimally invasive treatments.
